About Braillet

**Braillet** revolutionizes the creation of accessible signage and communication. We produce **small, durable, magnetic Braille blocks** that users can quickly arrange on a magnetic board to form any word or message.

This innovative system offers a cost-effective and flexible alternative to expensive, static Braille plates. For businesses and institutions, such as a school needing a flexible menu, Braillet provides a practical, reusable solution, saving significant time and money while ensuring full accessibility.

Accessibility made simple, affordable, and dynamic.
